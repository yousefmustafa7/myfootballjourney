My Football Journey

WELCOME

Welcome to 'My Football Journey'!

I'm Yousef Mustafa, and my world revolves around football. This website is my way of inviting you into my passion, from the streets of Amman, Jordan, where it all began, to my current home in Budapest, Hungary.

This is our space, where I'll be sharing the stories, the highs and lows, and the deep love I have for football. It's not just a game; it's a part of who I am, and I can't wait to have you alongside on this digital journey.

Lace up your virtual boots, and together, let's immerse ourselves in the beautiful world of football.

MY IDOL

Of course, it’s the Brazilian king.

Despite our differing playing styles, his electrifying skills and boundless love for the game have fueled my passion.

Watching Neymar in action is a thrill, and his journey has truly made me a die-hard football enthusiast. I was incredibly fortunate to witness him play in person at the 2022 World Cup, an experience that left an indelible mark on my football journey.
