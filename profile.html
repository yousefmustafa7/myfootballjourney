ABOUT ME

I was born in Amman, Jordan, the country's capital city, into a family that loved football as much as anyone in Jordan. I have always loved this sport and started playing in a football academy when I was 8 years old.

Ever since I was very young, I had dreamed of having a career in football. I grew up idolizing the Brazilian players, watching how they play the game in a way that makes whoever is watching them fall in love with the game. I was truly blessed to have a supportive family that helped me pursue my goals.

As I was growing up, I was playing for Al Ahli youth team, which plays in the first division of the Jordanian league. After just a few matches, I was lucky enough to be called up to the national team, which I definitely consider a great achievement for me.
